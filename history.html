History of Soccer

  1. The Origin, History, and Invention of Soccer

  2. The history of soccer also known as football spans thousands of years and includes many different games and rules.

  3. Modern soccerIn 1863, the Football Association was formed in England, standardizing the rules of the game and hosting the first cup competition In 1855, Charles Goodyear invented the first soccer ball made of vulcanized rubber In 1971, UEFA members voted to officially recognize women's football In 1991, the first FIFA Women's World Cup was held in China

  4. TodaySoccer is one of the most popular sports in the world, with millions of fans watching major tournaments like the World Cup The FIFA Women's World Cup has been held every four years since 1991
