How to play

  1. Players use their feet, head, or chest, except the goalie who can use their hands in their goal area
  2. The ball must cross the goal line to score
  3. The team with the most goals wins
  4. If the ball goes out of bounds, the team that didn't touch it last gets to throw it back in

Fouls

  1. Players can't kick, trip, charge, strike, push, hold, or spit at an opponent
  2. Bumping, leaning, or going shoulder-to-shoulder while competing for a ball is not a foul until the hands or elbows come up

Substitutions

  1. Substitutions can only be performed when the ball is not in play and your team has possession
  2. Youth leagues usually have no cap on substitutions

Disciplinary actions

  1. The referee issues red and yellow cards to players for infractions
  2. A red card immediately expels a player from the game
  3. Two yellow cards in the same game results in an automatic red card

Other rules

  1. The game begins at the center of the field
  2. The ball is in play unless it rolls across the touchline, the goal line, or the referee stops play
